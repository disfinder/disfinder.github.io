You wandered too long in the dark
And you thought that the night was the day
You saw hundreds of desecrated bodies
But were too lazy to lay them to rest
You're not a traitor, you're simply a fool
And there are millions like you all around
Poor Christ would have torn off his crown
Had he guessed what was yet to come...
...answer me
What kept you from seeing the truth?
What ever became of the dream
To make heaven on earth for all?

The wind tore the blindfold away from your face
And the light struck your eyes like a whip
It turned out a maniac had played the role of your father
And the people were obedient cattle
Everything changed in the eyes that now see
Who was a rat — became tiger and lion
You're not a traitor, you lived as a fool
And there are millions like you all around...
...answer me
What kept you from seeing the truth?
What ever became of the dream
To make heaven on earth for all?

Look back behind you
There the sunset stands frozen
Frozen in a black-and-red stripe
Ahead — only fog
Behind — only lies
What have you done with your dream?
