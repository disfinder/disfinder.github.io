The Americans have freedom,
The Germans — beer and bacon,
The French have haute couture,
The English — the finest stadium.

But your average Ukrainian
Excels at something else instead.
He has a long and elastic
Patience, like none you've seen.

Say those up top get a whim
To slap a tax on trousers —
The Ukrainian's patience won't snap,
Pull it for three hundred years.

Or the gang leads him astray:
Invites him to a bank — it's a brothel.
The Ukrainian will fetch his patience,
Sigh — and step on the rake again...

I've heard: the bosses, for a tranche,
Wanted to sell our patience off.
Don't sell it, I advise against it,
Or else you'll all get...
(...an unexpected and very
unpleasant ending).
