There is a fun resource that, in my opinion, is great for a laugh. It is useful in that it lets you personalize comments without creating a LiveJournal account. You open the page, find a suitable character, put on their mask, and voilà — now your comment is written not from some anonymous user, but from [Kenny](http://maskas.ru/maska/kenny), [Stallman](http://maskas.ru/maska/rms), [Bill Gates](http://maskas.ru/maska/bill_gates), or [whoever you fancy](http://maskas.ru/)
