It has always been a mystery to me: how do people in large institutions manage to finish their work so harmoniously that by 17:01 everyone is done and heading home? When a big team, like a single organism, rises and leaves the offices, creating traffic jams on the roads, queues in the stores…
It turns out it's very simple. This situation is typical of a team that does nothing.
