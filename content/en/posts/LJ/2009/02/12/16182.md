For the second week now I've been racking my brain trying to write a thesis abstract for my master's program. Since the university is very disorganized, I still don't have a supervisor for my master's thesis, and there's no one to ask for guidance. I've chosen the topic "Software of Automated Systems" and need to write two pages of abstracts for a conference presentation (apparently no actual presentation is required). And now I'm tormented by the agony of choice — which specific problems of software in automated systems should I write about in those two pages.
My thoughts categorically refuse to work in that direction :(
