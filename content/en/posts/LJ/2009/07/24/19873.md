failed to update … (unexpected error)

Might come in handy for Arch Linux newcomers.
If after updating pacman it starts showing the following:

:: Synchronizing package databases…
error: failed to update core (unexpected error)
error: failed to update extra (unexpected error)
error: failed to update community (unexpected error)
error: failed to synchronize any databases

don't panic — just edit /etc/pacman.d/mirrorlist
Uncomment the lines with the mirrors you use.
