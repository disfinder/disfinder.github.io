I'll just leave a note here for myself and posterity: on August 18th, 2025, at 22:30, I was woken up by the [beep of a smart flood sensor](/posts/2025/07/13/tapo-iot/), because for the third time this year water was pouring down on us from above.
<!--more-->

This time it wasn't a small trickle — it was a massive river. The pipe burst in the empty three-bedroom apartment upstairs, leaving three fingers of water standing on the floor, almost all of which poured down into our apartment.

Maybe I'll add photos someday, but for now we have to move out somewhere while repairs are done.

Life is a restless thing...
