So much has changed over the years of having that LiveJournal page.... Moving back to the homeland and back again. And another move. More moves that never got written about. Buying real estate and movable property. Significant and pleasant career changes, and even more significant — but far from pleasant — geopolitical changes....
<!--more-->
So I thought — against the backdrop of all these changes, some update to the terms of service (or whatever it's called) of LiveJournal is a minor thing. Let it stand, it'll fall on its own someday.

Especially since there was quite a lot of interesting stuff there — I wanted to share thoughts and observations, and some very smart people used to stop by. No, I won't delete it. Let it stay. Instead, maybe I'll keep these two blogs — the coherent one and the incoherent one — a little more active...
