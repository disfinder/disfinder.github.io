## Tiny Bins

[Tiny Bins - Efficient Small-Parts Storage [Parametric]](https://www.printables.com/model/701118-tiny-bins-efficient-small-parts-storage-parametric)

Wonderful small boxes for tiny parts that can be stacked inside larger boxes with lids, which in turn fit into a big holder. Super.
For this model I even "customized" a divider to split the smallest bins into 4 sections — and glued that divider into the bins.
