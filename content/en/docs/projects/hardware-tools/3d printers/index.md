## Bambulab A1 Mini

After playing around with the Ender, on the advice of friends I bought an [A1 mini](https://bambulab.com/en-us/a1-mini)

Side by side they looked like Wall-E and EVE from the WALL-E movie — such a comically apt resemblance to the visual and technological contrast between the two devices that I decided to name them accordingly.

And of course I immediately went for the combo — with AMS, the ability to print with 4 different filaments at once is a huge leap forward — on top of the fact that Bambu also prints several times faster.
